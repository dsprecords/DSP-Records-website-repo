What This Page Is

DnB_Culture is a platform being built to grow and exhibit drum & bass video culture — long-form video mixes, filmed sets, and visual projects built around the music.

Leadership

This is Dan Snakehead’s page — the host of Nocturnal Emissions Radio. Dan leads the direction, selection, and execution of what appears here.

DSP Role

DSP Records is collaborating on Dan’s project by providing hosting, infrastructure, and amplification — without administering the page or taking credit for the project.

Alignment

Dan’s values, beliefs, and objectives are fully aligned with the reform DSP wants to see in the scene — culture-first, respect-first, and real contribution over hype.

Transparency: Hosted under DSP for practicality — not ownership. Dan maintains creative control and administration.
