About

DSP Records (Dyrtie Sound Productions) is an independent underground electronic music label focused on bass-driven sounds built for dark rooms, loud systems, and real connection.

Founded to support artists and community over hype, DSP Records is committed to long-term growth, creative freedom, and reinvesting back into the scene.
