About

About DSP Records DSP Records is an independent, artist-first underground electronic music label built for dark rooms, loud systems, and real connection. Founded from years in the scene, DSP exists to support music that’s made with intention — not algorithms. We focus on bass-driven underground sounds, raw energy, and releases that are meant to be felt on proper systems, not rushed for trends. This label is structured around respect for the craft, fair treatment of artists, and long-term growth. We prioritize creative freedom, sustainable releases, and reinvesting back into the music and the community that surrounds it. DSP Records isn’t about hype cycles or shortcuts. It’s about consistency, momentum, and building something real — one release, one connection, one system at a time.
