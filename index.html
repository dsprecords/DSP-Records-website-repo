About

DSP Records is an independent underground electronic music label built for dark rooms, loud systems, and real connection.

Founded from years in the scene, DSP exists to protect and push the music itself — the culture, the energy, and the unity that happens when sound, space, and people align.

DSP Records isn’t about hype cycles or shortcuts. It’s about consistency, momentum, and building something real — one release, one connection, one system at a time.
